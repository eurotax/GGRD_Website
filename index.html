Fair-launch path

How the Meteora DBC Launch Works

GGRD uses a public Dynamic Bonding Curve (DBC) on Meteora instead of private seed rounds. There is no presale and no team allocation off-chain ‚Äì everyone joins directly on-chain under the same rules.

  1. Prepare a Solana wallet (for example Phantom or Backpack).
  2. Fund it with SOL and USDC on Solana.
  3. When the fair launch begins, this page and @GGRDofficial / @ContactGiggle will publish the official DBC and token mint addresses.
  4. You will be able to buy GGRD directly from the curve using USDC until the migration threshold (around 80,000 USDC in curve TVL) is reached.
  5. After migration, liquidity is moved to a standard AMM pool (for example via Jupiter / Raydium) and trading continues there with 0% tax.

Charity Treasury

10% Meme Supply, Real Impact ‚Äì Tabletochki Only

The GGRD Charity Treasury is a dedicated on-chain wallet holding 100,000 GGRD (10% of total supply). It is managed by a 3/4 Squads multisig and has exactly one purpose: supporting Ukrainian children with cancer and their families, especially those affected by war.

Under the Donation Policy, the only beneficiary of this treasury is Tabletochki Charity Foundation. Funds cannot be used for team incentives, marketing, liquidity, CEX fees or any profit-oriented activity. The treasury may only:

  • swap GGRD into assets accepted by Tabletochki (e.g. USDC),
  • send on-chain donations to official Tabletochki wallets / payment gateways.

Planned 10-Month Donation Schedule

After the DBC migrates to an LP and trading stabilises, the Charity Treasury follows a roughly 10-month programme:

  • each month a defined portion of GGRD is swapped to USDC,
  • swaps respect strict daily and monthly volume caps versus average trading volume,
  • at the end of each month, a single on-chain donation is sent to Tabletochki,
  • every transaction is published in public transparency reports.
Transparency: Every swap and donation will be traceable on-chain, with summaries linking the Charity Treasury multisig and the official Tabletochki infrastructure.
Disclaimer: Tabletochki Charity Foundation does not promote, manage or recommend the GGRD token or any related products. All donations are unilateral decisions of the GGRD project.
